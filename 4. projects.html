Projects


Our Upcoming Documentary


We are making a documentary series going through the different livestock and how they are prepared to be sold in stores. The video below is a short snippet of our chicken epsiode. The meat packing industry is quite cruel and we plan on showing you a little bit of what its like. Methods such as steroids, antibiotics, and electrocution are used in the meatpacking industry. This treatment is unacceptable and needs to be changed. We plan on making this series to inform people so that we can have more humane forms of bringing meat products to our stores.



As you can see in the images above our cameramen work very hard to ensure you all get the information you need to make an informed decision on how you feel about the current situation.


Operation Green


For the month of August, our reserachers have started Operation Green! This new effort focuses on the greenery surrounding farms that farm animals are able to roam. We have research teams that each go to different farms to assess the well-being of the animals. They also bring along state-of-the-art technology to make sure the animals are in good health and to determine how the farmers care for their animals. Our teams take there time at each location making sure anything and everything is looked over and accounted for to make sure that the farms are using the most humane techniques for their livestock.


Hero Helpers


Our Hero Helpers project allows everyone from different communities to gather and help our organization! From assisting farmhands to planting plants, our Hero Helpers create major change. Animals deserve a good life and our Hero Helpers are here to make sure of that! We partner with PETA volunteers.
