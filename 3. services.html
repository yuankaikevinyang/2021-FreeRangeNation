Services





Retraining Employees

Every month we send out a team to educate farmers on proper animal care and any updated care requirements posted by the USDA. We also reach out to hundreds of meatpacking factories monthly to educate workers on proper and humane practices in the treatment of animals.

Researching

Our researchers visit farms and meatpacking factories to assess the wellness of animals and utilize state-of-the-art technology to find alternative methods to promote the free-range mindset.


Community Service

Qualifying schools, organizations, and programs are able to request for community service volunteer opportunities from us! Just contact us and we can get your group started.

Education

Free Range Nation has many educational tools for everyone to access and learn about the world of humane animal treatment. Contact us through our website, email, or phone and we can send you our educational resources!
