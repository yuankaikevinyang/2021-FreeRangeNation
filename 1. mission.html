Mission & Vision



Our Mission

The Free Range Nation is an organization that prioritizes the safety of livestock and ensure that they are being treated humanely. Some issues we focus on include combating the use of electrocution, antibiotics, steroids, and among other inhumane practices; and we accomplish this through means of retraining employees, creating documentaries to educate the public on the reality of the meatpacking industry, and much more.

Most of the nine billion livestock in the U.S. are confined in concentrated animal feeding operations (CAFOs). The extreme confinement practices such as battery cages, continue to be a standard practice despite being outlawed in a dozen states.


Our Vision

Free Range Nation is looking for a future of animal welfare, a world where animals are able to live out peaceful lives. We want to eliminate inhumane treatment of all animals.
