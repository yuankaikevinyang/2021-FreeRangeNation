Our Team & Partners


Meet Our Team


Dr. Leela Hazzah

Chief Executive Officer and President

Hazzah is an Egyptian conservation biologist, entrepreneur, and animal rights activist who loves lions and helped started Lion guardians, a non-profit in East Africa. The organization works to ensure safe and sustainable solutions for animal/human conflict. Has received several awards for being the Director and Founding member of Women for Environment Africa and Pride Lion Conservation Alliance. Click on her picture to learn more.

Michelle Cho

Chief Development and Marketing Officer

Passion for animals to develop impactful campaigns rights.Served as the Associate Director of Communication at PETA for several years. Regularly engages celebrities to lend their voices to her campaigns to create social impact and cross-sector partnerships. Click on her picture to learn more.

Jane Velez Mitchell

Chief International Ofiicer

Former CNN anchor and the founder also editor of JaneUnChained News, a non-profit, social media news network reporting on animal rights, veganism, health, and climate change. Honored for fighting animal abuse by the Animal legal defense Fund. Click on her picture to learn more.

Genesis Butler

Media Spokesperson

She was the youngest TEDx speaker who gave a speech about veganism and its positive effect on the environment. She also campaigns for animals using social media and started Genesis For Animals. Won several awards such as the Animal Hero Kid's paul Mc Cartney Young Veg Advocate Award. Click on her picture to learn more.
